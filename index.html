Our Founder

Rohit Ravinder, the founder of TapAcademy and a highly experienced educator with over 10 years in the field.

Rohit founded TapAcademy with the goal of making coding education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or prior experience. He recognized that traditional teaching methods could be dry and unengaging, so he set out to create a new way of teaching that would make coding fun and interactive. With TapAcademy, Rohit has achieved this goal by using augmented reality, animations, and visualizations to bring coding to life.
